Language Skills
For convenience, here is a list of common criteria for rating language ability.
As the area of the graphs below fills, 'native-equivalent' speaking ability is achieved.
0 - No Practical Proficiency
1 - Elementary Proficiency
2 - Limited Working Proficiency
3 - Minimum Professional Proficiency
4 - Full Professional Proficiency
5 - Native or Bilingual Proficiency
Listening: the ability to follow and understand speech in the language.
Speaking: the ability to produce speech in the language and be understood by its speakers.
Reading: the ability to read and understand texts written in the language.
Writing: the ability to formulate written texts in the language.
