At first, I remember wanting to play a string instrument, but I ended up playing Trumpet in 4th grade instead. I was really quite good, and was better than my peers at that age. As I got older, I continued to excel, participating in band and being the 1st Chair Trumpet player most of my public school life. In college I learned Cello, and now I am trying to find more time to practice both of those musical instruments. I also want to learn more.
I feel really relaxed when I play music. I also really like listening to music and analyzing it. It's definitely something that makes me feel happy.
As I grew older, I learned that I didn't like to be pressured into playing music. It is one of the reasons I never sought a profession as a musician. But, I am excited that music is important to humanity because it is important to me, too.
In college, I mainly used music as a relief for me.
The whole experience of music is something that I want to continue. I also want to make it easier for others to enjoy music, and I realize that I have had more luck with having parents who appreciate music and encouraged me to try it out.
